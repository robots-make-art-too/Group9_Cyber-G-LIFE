People in remote and impoverished parts of Africa are always struggling to survive.
Famines, droughts, and diseases are happening by their sides,
threatening their lives like a nightmare.

Clean water resources and a basic living environment is within reach for us
however a luxury for them. Unfortunately, The world's eyes seldom
focus on these people who have been in need.

There are many kinds of suffering, but the trauma of war is the most incapacitating wound.
Insignificant people have become victims of uselessness due to
the wrestling between nations.

Innocent people have witnessed their homes burn into ruins, and their fragile lives
were taken away by the artillery fire by day and night. Lingering survivors
struggled to stumble through the ruins, but never
know the war when to subside.
